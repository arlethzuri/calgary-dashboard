## Developing an ontology using Methontology (Fernandez et. al)

### Domain: 
Energy and Smart Cities

### Date: 
2/5/2026

### Purpose: 
An ontology about city electrical infrastructure and human population and demographics. To be used to visualize locations of city/electrical infrastructure of interest and human presence. This will enable policy makers to answer questions about where electrical needs are and how human and city activity use electricity.

### Level of Formality: 
Semi-formal

### Scope: 
The city of Calgary using data available from 2020-2026 (ideally to present day)

### Data sources: 
Calgary Open Data portal, Canadian federal government, Calgary electric utility companies.

### Knowledge sources:
DGRI Lab, WIRED Global Center collaborators, relevant research articles and related ontologies (to be listed/cited).
